Name: Caesar Salad
Ingredients: 1 head Romaine Lettuce, 1/2 cup Croutons, 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese, 3 tbsp Caesar Dressing
Instructions: Chop lettuce and mix with croutons and parmesan cheese. Add Caesar dressing and toss well before serving.
Taste: Savory
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cuisine: Mediterranean
Reviews: 4.6
