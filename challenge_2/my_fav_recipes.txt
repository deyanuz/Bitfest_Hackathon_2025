Name: Pancakes
Ingredients: 2 cups Flour, 1 cup Milk, 2 pcs Egg, 2 tbsp Sugar, 1 tsp Baking Powder
Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Heat a skillet and pour batter. Cook on both sides until golden. Serve warm with syrup.
Taste: Sweet
Preparation Time: 15 minutes
Cuisine: American
Reviews: 4.5


Name: Spaghetti Bolognese
Ingredients: 200 grams Spaghetti, 300 grams Ground Beef, 1 cup Tomato Sauce, 1 tbsp Olive Oil, 1 clove Garlic
Instructions: Cook spaghetti as per package instructions. In a pan, sauté garlic in olive oil, add beef, and cook. Mix in tomato sauce and combine with spaghetti.
Taste: Savory
Preparation Time: 30 minutes
Cuisine: Italian
Reviews: 4.7


Name: Chicken Curry
Ingredients: 500 grams Chicken, 2 cups Coconut Milk, 2 tbsp Curry Powder, 1 Onion, 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
Instructions: Sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste. Add chicken and curry powder, cook until brown. Add coconut milk and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
Taste: Spicy
Preparation Time: 40 minutes
Cuisine: Indian
Reviews: 4.8


Name: Caesar Salad
Ingredients: 1 head Romaine Lettuce, 1/2 cup Croutons, 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese, 3 tbsp Caesar Dressing
Instructions: Chop lettuce and mix with croutons and parmesan cheese. Add Caesar dressing and toss well before serving.
Taste: Savory
Preparation Time: 10 minutes
Cuisine: Mediterranean
Reviews: 4.6


Name: Chocolate Chip Cookies
Ingredients: 2 cups Flour, 1 cup Butter, 1 cup Sugar, 2 pcs Egg, 1 cup Chocolate Chips, 1 tsp Vanilla Extract, 1 tsp Baking Soda
Instructions: Cream butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix in flour, baking soda, and chocolate chips. Drop by spoonfuls onto baking sheet and bake at 180°C for 10-12 minutes.
Taste: Sweet
Preparation Time: 25 minutes
Cuisine: American
Reviews: 4.9


Name: Lemonade
Ingredients: 4 Lemons (juiced), 1/2 cup Sugar, 4 cups Water, Ice Cubes, Lemon Slices (for garnish)
Instructions: In a pitcher, mix lemon juice, sugar, and water until sugar dissolves. Add ice cubes and garnish with lemon slices. Serve chilled.
Taste: Sweet
Preparation Time: 5 minutes
Cuisine: American
Reviews: 4.6

Name: Vegetable Stir-Fry
Ingredients: 2 cups Mixed Vegetables (Carrots, Broccoli, Bell Peppers), 2 tbsp Soy Sauce, 1 tbsp Sesame 0il, 1 clove Garlic, 1 tsp Ginger
Instructions: Heat sesame oil in a wok. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for 1 minute. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes. Add soy sauce and mix well. Serve hot.
Taste: Savory
Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Reviews: 4.7

Name: Banana Smoothie
Ingredients: 2 ripe Bananas, 1 cup Milk, 1 tbsp Honey, 1/2 tsp vanilla Extract, Ice Cubes (optional)
Instructions: Combine bananas, milk, honey, and vanilla in a blender. Blend until smooth. Add ice cubes if desired. Serve chilled.
Taste: Sweet
Preparation Time: 5 minutes
Cuisine: American
Reviews: 4.5
